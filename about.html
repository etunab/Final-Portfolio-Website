About me

Etuna Bekauri is a Communication Design Student at NYC College Of Technology. Within three years, she mastered important design skills such as using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, InDesign, etc. She was simultaneously working with the fashion magazine “Presage” and did magazine covers, mockups, and press kits for them. She currently works as a graphic design intern through the Brooklyn Navy Yard program at High Fidelity company. She loves creative work and is willing to help companies achieve their goals.
